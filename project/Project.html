What's Artificial Intelligence?






Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on building intelligent systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding natural language, and recognizing patterns.

AI systems rely on data, algorithms, and computational power to analyze information and make predictions or decisions. Through machine learning and deep learning techniques, AI can continuously improve its performance without being explicitly programmed.

Today, AI plays a vital role in modern technology, powering smart assistants, recommendation systems, medical diagnostics, autonomous vehicles, and much more. As AI continues to evolve, it is expected to reshape industries, enhance productivity, and transform the way humans interact with machines.
